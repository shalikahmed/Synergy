ABOUT

SYNERGY

The interactive platform for students is an idea to enable students to provide knowledge that they actually require. This project allows the student to upload questions in the platform and get answers for them. The questions can be of the form of text or image for which they would get answers from other people who are registered in the platform. Every student of all years can create an account in this platform for posting questions or providing solutions for them. This platform can also be utilized to post new ideas of students like project ideas or sharing any technical information which would be useful for other people.
